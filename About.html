OUR HISTORY


Excellent Nursery and Primary School started as a private mixed school with classes; baby to Primary Seven respectively but with out a UNEB center for P.L.E examinations.



By 2023, Excellent Nursery and Primary School had acquired all the basic requirements for the licence and registration by the Minstry of Education ans Sports, Registration No PPS/E/121.
